What does FHDHGNGN Mean?

FHDHGNGN stands for nothing. When I made the username, I was playing a Terraria (or at the time I thought, Minecraft) knockoff mobile game called The Blockheads. I was joining online play for the first time and needed a username. You guessed it! I keyboard smashed.

The result was very different from FHDHGNGN, that's because after I deleted the game, I forgot the username (what a surprise) but the next time I needed a username, I just tried remembering what my username was for The Blockheads. It came out as FHDHGNGN. Ever since late 2016/early 2017, it has been my go-to username until 2018. Here are all the username changes I have done.

2015-2016: xboxgolfer
2016-2018: FHDHGNGNgaming
2018-2019: FHDgaming
2019-2019: FHD
2019-2020: FyreBoye
2020-2020: Robut
2020-2020: Roebut444/Roebut4
2020-Present: anthonyhuang07/FHDHGNGN

Also, I lied. FHDHGNGN has some meaning (kinda?? I just made it up like 2 years after I made the username) the reason why I switched to FHD later on was not only to simplify it because nobody could remember it, but I just looked up a meaning for FHD and it actually meant Full HD, which is 1080p. Very fitting at the time because I only made 1080p videos. The rest means nothing.

If you want to memorize the username. You can think of FHDHGNGN standing for "Full High-Definition (HD) h GoodNightGoodNight"
The GNGN doesn't actually mean goodnightgoodnight, but it's a nice way to remember it. You start with the name for 1080p (Full HD), then add the middle letter in FHD (h, or simply just remember h because of the meme) then add Goodnight Goodnight. And there you have it, FHDHGNGN!
